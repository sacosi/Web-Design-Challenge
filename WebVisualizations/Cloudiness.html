Cloudiness

In this plot we are taking a closer look at how the Cloudiness of a City varies depending on how far or close it is from the equator. This is a very interesting plot. The Latitude seems to not have a big impact on the cloudiness value. However, at the time of the year that this study was performed, there seems to be a comon values of cloudiness for interval of 20% with the exception of the 60% mark.
